The Production

A Legend
Reborn

· · ·

Written in the 16th century, Journey to the West is one of the most cherished works in world literature — a mythological epic beloved by billions across China, India, Southeast Asia, and Australia. At its heart is Sun Wukong, the indomitable Monkey King: warrior, trickster, and hero.

"A myth reborn for the digital age. A celebration of collaboration, identity, and global storytelling." — Kerry Jewel, Producer & Creator

Now, for the first time, this legendary story will be brought to life as a US$15 million immersive stage production — fusing animatronics, acrobatics, digital projection, and original music in a groundbreaking theatrical experience designed for global audiences.

Creative Vision

"This is not traditional theatre.
This is a live-action, immersive world."

Featuring immersive digital environments, animatronic and practical effects, original composition and choreography, and an international cast — Monkey King redefines what a stage production can be.
