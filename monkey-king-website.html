Executive Summary

Monkey King: Journey to the West is a groundbreaking, immersive stage production based on the most iconic story in Chinese literature. Blending ancient mythology with state-of-the-art technology, this show is designed to become a global franchise and cultural milestone. This whitepaper outlines the creative vision, business model, market strategy, and financial opportunity for investors.

In an original and unique arrangement, the world's first fully insured entertainment investment. Fully insured against loss by H2 Entertainment Insurance Solutions.

Franchise Potential & Risk Diversification

Monkey King: Journey to the West is more than a stage show— it’s a scalable IP universe with the potential for: Merchandise lines, Animated and live-action TV / Film adaptations, Theme park licensing, Web3 collectibles and gamified digital assets.

Revenue is diversified across: Ticket sales, Licensing, Merchandising, Digital rights, Long-term spin-offs. This model ensures the project can weather fluctuating sales while maintaining profitability via subsidiary ventures.

All derivative revenue streams follow a 60% Investor / 40% Producer revenue split model framework.
