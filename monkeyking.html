EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

A groundbreaking theatrical production designed to become a global entertainment franchise.

Monkey King: Journey to the West is a groundbreaking immersive stage production inspired by one of the most iconic stories in Chinese literature.

Blending mythology, cinematic storytelling, immersive technology, live theatre, acrobatics, animatronics, and digital projections, the production is positioned as both a premium entertainment experience and scalable global intellectual property.
